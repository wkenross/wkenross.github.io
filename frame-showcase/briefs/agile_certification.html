Agile Delivery And Certification

Purpose

Show how FRAME uses Agile artifacts to improve the automation framework and prepare for certification.

Working Model

  • product backlog
  • epics
  • user stories
  • acceptance criteria
  • two-week sprints
  • sprint reviews
  • retrospectives
  • Jira sandbox when available

Certification Tie

PSM I is the first Agile certification target. Jira certification becomes a tool-specific follow-up after the personal Jira sandbox has real board and sprint-report evidence.

Evidence To Publish

  • scrubbed backlog example
  • one sprint goal
  • completed story summary
  • retrospective improvement
  • certification-readiness map

Do not publish private Jira URLs, account identifiers, or screenshots that expose tenant state.
