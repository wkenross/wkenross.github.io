B2B/B2C Solutions Showcase

FRAME is shaped by product-management work on real B2B and B2C portal solutions: insurance risk and vehicle valuation, economic data management, energy operations, API delivery, analytics, and Agile delivery.

The public story is not that FRAME exposes private systems. The public story is that the same operating pattern can be reused safely:

  1. Capture requirements and background evidence.
  2. Review and obfuscate private details.
  3. Convert product work into PlanMe, TrainMe, CodeMe, RunMe, backlog, and

    4. Jira-ready artifacts.

  4. Use SQL, BI, CRM, browser automation, and scripting only after the workflow

    5. is clear.

  5. Publish public-safe summaries, not raw client material.

Why This Belongs At The Top Of The Showcase

The B2B/B2C solutions section explains where the automation framework came from. These are not isolated demos. They are the business/product situations that drove the need for repeatable automation:

  • product requirements from complex portals
  • API and data-delivery decisions
  • customer and public-sector workflows
  • migration from older product patterns to modern SaaS
  • Agile backlog and release discipline
  • product analytics and executive reporting
  • AI-assisted synthesis tied to traceable inputs, human checkpoints, and reviewed next actions

Generative AI Influence

FRAME now pairs the product lessons from B2B/B2C portals with hands-on generative AI workflows. AI is used to summarize ambiguous inputs, structure recommendation lists, draft reviewed artifacts, compare requirements against skills and experience, and prepare next-step wizards.

The public positioning should stay practical: generative AI accelerates product discovery, requirements analysis, documentation, data review, and operator preparation. It should not be described as unattended applying, sending, publishing, or account-changing automation.
