BI Reporting Case Study Candidate

Problem

Business reporting often starts in Excel but needs reproducible source data, repeatable refresh, and tool-specific reporting outputs.

FRAME Pattern

  1. SQL workflow produces synthetic, reporting-ready CSV exports.
  2. BI workflow stages those exports with a manifest.
  3. Power BI and Tableau briefs are generated from the same data.
  4. The operator builds reports manually in the BI tools.
  5. Evidence review blocks sharing until artifacts are inspected.

Public-Safe Evidence

Use screenshots or PDFs generated from synthetic data only. Keep workbook files private until inspected for embedded paths, credentials, account state, and data.
