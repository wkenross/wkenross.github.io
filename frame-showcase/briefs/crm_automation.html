Salesforce Cloud CRM Planning

Purpose

Present the CRM automation lane around Salesforce cloud while keeping legacy HubSpot work useful as migration context.

Platform Direction

Primary path:

  • Salesforce cloud CRM
  • Trailhead and Developer Edition practice
  • Salesforce Platform Administrator certification
  • Sales Cloud Consultant follow-up
  • Trailblazer profile as Salesforce-native personal evidence

Secondary/context path:

  • generic HubSpot personal subscription workflows
  • HubSpot-to-Salesforce concept mapping
  • Outlook Classic draft-first email handling
  • Word document intake from sales and marketing

Website Treatment

CRM pages should use the shared FRAME style. They should not mimic Salesforce or HubSpot UI. Use generic platform names, generic object labels, and synthetic contacts. Link to a Trailblazer profile only after the profile is intentionally public and reviewed.

Evidence To Build

  • Salesforce Account and Contact mapping
  • Lead, Campaign, Campaign Member, Task, Case, and Opportunity examples
  • synthetic import-ready CSVs
  • validation reports before upload
  • certification readiness map
  • public-safe Trailblazer/FRAME site positioning

Publish Rules

  • no real HubSpot or Salesforce URLs
  • no account screenshots unless scrubbed
  • no real contact names or emails
  • no employer-specific page titles
  • no send-by-default email automation examples
  • no Salesforce credentials or Trailhead claims until earned
