FEDM Solution Brief

FEDM is the B2B SaaS modernization case study for moving FAME economic data management from a client-server heritage toward a hosted SaaS model with RESTful navigation, API complements, validation workflows, and Agile delivery.

Product Story

The solution demonstrates:

  • migration from legacy client-server product patterns to SaaS delivery
  • browser-based navigation for economic data discovery and management
  • API and connector complements for integration and automation
  • economic warehouse validation, data catalog, and metadata discipline
  • release/demo practices and the transition from waterfall habits to Agile
