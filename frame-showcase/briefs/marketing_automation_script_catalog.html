Review Rule

These examples do not connect to live websites, mailboxes, CRM systems, or marketplace accounts. They are designed to show the architecture using synthetic data.

Before publication, confirm that the files contain:

  • no private paths
  • no account names
  • no real client or employer data
  • no live platform selectors
  • no send, submit, or profile-update action
