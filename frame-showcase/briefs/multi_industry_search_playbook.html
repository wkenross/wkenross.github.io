Multi-Industry Search Playbook

FRAME discovery workflows are most useful when the same structure can be tuned for different markets.

The operator changes the source list and scoring lens, but keeps the core automation pattern:

  1. collect source cards
  2. normalize fields
  3. score fit and urgency
  4. generate a recommendation list
  5. open a reviewed action wizard
  6. track follow-up and outcomes

Themed Source Sets

Each lens can use its own source set:

  • public RFP sites
  • freelance and contractor marketplaces
  • industry association pages
  • partner directories
  • CRM exports
  • saved search pages
  • reviewed email alerts

The point is not to scrape everything. The point is to make repeated research reviewable, explainable, and easier to resume.

Recommendation Output

A strong recommendation list should show:

  • why the opportunity was selected
  • what industry lens matched
  • what evidence or service offering supports it
  • what the risk is
  • what the next reviewed action should be

This gives the operator a short, useful working list instead of another unstructured pile of links.
