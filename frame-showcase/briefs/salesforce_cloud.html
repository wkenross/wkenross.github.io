Salesforce Cloud CRM Evidence

Purpose

Position the CRM showcase around Salesforce cloud, Trailhead learning, and certification-ready practice.

HubSpot remains useful as migration context and a comparison point for marketing email workflows. Salesforce is the primary public CRM story because it supports cloud CRM data modeling, Sales Cloud process language, Service Cloud-style case thinking, Trailhead evidence, and recognized credentials.

First Certification Path

Primary:

  • Salesforce Certified Platform Administrator

Follow-up:

  • Salesforce Certified Sales Cloud Consultant

The Sales Cloud Consultant path should wait until Administrator readiness and hands-on Salesforce cloud evidence are real.
