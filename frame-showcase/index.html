FRAME Evidence Workbench

FRAME is a review-first automation workbench for product management, sales analytics, marketing operations, business intelligence, agile delivery, and AI-assisted workflow preparation.

The showcase is organized around one practical idea: turn messy source material into reviewable evidence, structured recommendations, dashboards, backlog items, and next-step wizards while keeping final send, submit, publish, import, and account-changing actions under human control.

Framework Sources

FRAME grew from product-management work on B2B and B2C portal solutions, then expanded into generative AI, data/BI, CRM, and desktop automation patterns.

Source What It Shows Start Here
Energy Portal B2B operations across dashboards, contracts, counterparties, carriers, transactions, invoices, executive reports, and revenue modeling Energy Portal
Price Digests Insurance risk workflows, valuation products, API delivery, product analytics, and backlog discipline Price Digests
FEDM SaaS migration, economic data management, REST/API complements, validation, and Agile transition FEDM
AI Tooling Requirements synthesis, recommendation scoring, reviewed artifacts, browser/email preparation, and safety gates AI Tooling
