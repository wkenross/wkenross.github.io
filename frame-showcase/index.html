FRAME Showcase Introduction Refresh

FRAME is a practical automation workbench for reviewed product, sales, business intelligence, and operations workflows.

The showcase should make the working pattern easy to understand:

  1. Collect public-safe source material or structured input files.
  2. Normalize the content into useful cards, rows, or requirements.
  3. Score and sort the work into recommendation lists.
  4. Use interactive wizards to prepare the next step.
  5. Keep the operator in control of submissions, outreach, publication, and

    6. account-changing actions.

Featured B2B/B2C Portal Solutions

FRAME also has a product-management story: it grew from real B2B and B2C portal work where product requirements, data quality, automation, stakeholder review, and reporting had to fit together.

Showcase Lanes

Product Management

FRAME supports product owner, Agile delivery, requirements, roadmap, and stakeholder workflows. It can convert opportunity requirements into resume variants, cover letters, interview prep, gap notes, and backlog-style planning.

Sales Automation

FRAME supports public-safe lead discovery, RFP intake, lead-list grooming, CRM-ready reports, and reviewed follow-up. The public story should emphasize recommendation lists and guided next steps rather than unattended outreach.

Business Intelligence

FRAME supports Excel dashboard planning, SQL and data cleanup workflows, Power BI and Tableau-oriented requirements, and reporting packages that make metrics easier to inspect and refresh.

Mobile-To-Desktop Handoff

The fr_mobile workflow lets iPhone notes and recommendations become Windows workflow requirements. This gives the system a way to capture requirements on the road and process them later through the appropriate workspace.

Generative AI + Review-First Automation

FRAME uses generative AI to summarize ambiguous inputs, compare requirements against skills and experience, draft reviewed artifacts, structure recommendation lists, and prepare wizard-driven next steps. The key operating principle is practical control: AI accelerates product discovery, documentation, data review, and preparation, while a person remains responsible for final submissions, outreach, publication, and account-changing actions.
