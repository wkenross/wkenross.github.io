FRAME

FRAME is my working automation framework for product, sales, BI, and operations workflows. It combines web research, email signals, spreadsheet data, resume and proposal material, recommendation lists, and operator-guided wizards.

The emphasis is practical and review-first: automation prepares the work, organizes the evidence, and opens the next step, while a person stays in control of decisions, submissions, outreach, and publication.

Interactive Wizards

FRAME wizards are reviewed action managers. They can open the right page, copy the right path, prepare a draft, organize notes, or route an inbox item. They are not positioned as unattended submitters or email senders.
